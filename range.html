Gun Rentals

We have a large selection of firearms for rent.

We have many pistols, rifles, and shotguns available; come check out our selection!

Rental fee for any firearm is $25, plus ammunition. Or $45, plus ammunition to try as many available firearms as you like!

*Rental firearms are yours to use for the duration of your lane rental.

Policies and Restrictions

You or a member of your group must posses a valid Nebraska Handgun Purchase Permit, a Concealed Handgun Permit, or an active duty military ID to rent a firearm for use on the range.

If you do not yet have one of these permits or a military ID, you may still legally rent firearms if you are supervised by a certified instructor for the duration of your rental. We always have a qualified instructor on staff who will be happy to help you.

The discounted instruction fee for 30 minutes of rental supervision is $39.

People who are prohibited from owning or possessing firearms may not rent from us under any circumstances. Prohibited persons include: convicted felons, those with domestic violence or illegal substances convictions, and others. Please speak with an Athena Arms professional for more information.
